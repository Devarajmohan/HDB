SEO Process Flow Chart Steps

Website Analysis (Audit)

  • Objective: To evaluate your website’s current performance and identify areas for improvement.
  • Activities:
    • Analyze site speed, usability, and mobile-friendliness.
    • Identify technical issues such as broken links, crawl errors, or duplicate content.
    • Audit existing content for quality, relevance, and SEO effectiveness.
    • Check indexing status and ensure search engines can properly crawl the site.
  • Outcome: A detailed report highlighting technical, structural, and content issues that need to be fixed.

Keyword Research

  • Objective:To identify the best keywords for targeting your audience and driving relevant traffic.
  • Activities:
    • Analyze site speed, usability, and mobile-friendliness.
    • Identify technical issues such as broken links, crawl errors, or duplicate content.
    • Audit existing content for quality, relevance, and SEO effectiveness.
    • Check indexing status and ensure search engines can properly crawl the site.
  • Outcome: A detailed report highlighting technical, structural, and content issues that need to be fixed.

On-Page Optimization

  • Objective:To optimize website elements for better visibility in search engine results.
  • Activities:
    • Optimize meta tags: titles, descriptions, and headers with target keywords.
    • Improve URL structure (e.g., short, descriptive URLs with keywords).
    • Implement proper internal linking to improve navigation and site authority.
    • Ensure images have alt tags and are optimized for faster loading.
  • Outcome:A fully optimized website that aligns with search engine guidelines.

Content Creation

  • Objective:To produce high-quality, relevant, and engaging content that attracts users and search engines.
  • Activities:
    • Write blogs, articles, infographics, and videos targeting identified keywords.
    • Focus on E-E-A-T (Experience, Expertise, Authority, and Trustworthiness) in all content.
    • Refresh and update old content to maintain relevance.
    • Create cornerstone content that establishes authority on key topics.
  • Outcome:A fully optimized website that aligns with search engine guidelines.

Off-Page Optimization

  • Objective:To build your website’s authority and trustworthiness through external activities.
  • Activities:
    • Acquire high-quality backlinks from authoritative websites.
    • Engage in guest posting and outreach to industry blogs.
    • List your business in relevant directories and forums.
    • Leverage social media platforms for content sharing and engagement.
  • Outcome:Enhanced domain authority and improved trust in the eyes of search engines.
